LENS TECH FEATURES

See life in HD. Polarization is essential in keeping your eyes comfortable all day by reducing glare reflected off objects around you. Polarized lenses remove glare reflected off of flat, smooth or shiny surfaces and enhances colors and definition. Now your eyes can relax, so kick back and enjoy the rest of the day.

Ultra violet rays are extremely harmful to your eyes. All Crush Sunglasses provide UV400 protection, blocking over 99% of UVA and UVB light rays. The Ultraviolet light spectrum is from 100 nanometers to 400nm. UV400 lenses block up to 400nm of light. Above 400nm is the visible light spectrum.

Our polycarbonate lenses offer distortion free clarity and are impact-resistant, making them a great choice for your most extreme activities.

FRAME TECH FEATURES

All Crush frames are made with 100% virgin Grade-A materials. All frames are precision engineered and designed for a premium comfort fit. Hinges include 3 or 5 barrel, spring and interlocking.
