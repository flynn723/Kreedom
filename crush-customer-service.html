HOW SOON WILL I RECEIVE MY ORDER?

We ship all orders within 2 business days of receipt and ship most orders USPS 1st Class mail which typically takes 2-5 business days depending on your location. If you select USPS Priority Mail during checkout, shipment should take 2-3 business days after shipment.

HOW CAN I TRACK MY ORDER?

We will send you an email confirmation with tracking information to the address provided once your order has shipped.
